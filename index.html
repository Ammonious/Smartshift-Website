Smart Shift Pro

Mobile Temp-Employee Time Management

Smart. Easy. Efficient.

Improve workflow with Smart Shift Pro's time management system. Clients can easily record, track, and submit time cards without having to fill out form after form.

Smart Shift Pro leverages the power of technology to boost your clients productivity, reduce data entry, and save you the two most valuable things for your business - Time and Money.

Information when clients need it.

Clients will never miss a beat. No need to dig through piles of paperwork if they don't remember crucial information. Instead, quickly search for the employee and pull up work dates, whether that time card was submitted, and more!

Intelligent time entry.

Smart Shift eliminates common mistakes by calculating the hours for each temp employee's shift. Clients can review, edit and verify all time cards before submission.

Increased security and accountability.

All time card entries require an authorized signature reducing the risk of forged time cards.

Quick & Easy Setup

Getting started is easy! Clients simply select create account on the login screen, enter their given employer ID number, a few credentials, and they're all set!
